## Overview

Finding the right co-founder is one of the biggest challenges in launching a successful startup. Many entrepreneurs struggle to connect with compatible partners who share their vision, skills, and commitment.

To solve this, **Founder Matching** was developed as an AI-powered platform that **intelligently connects entrepreneurs** based on their skills, business interests, and personal compatibility. The platform leverages **data-driven matching techniques** to analyze user-provided data, ensuring optimal matching results. It considers factors such as expertise, industry focus, personality traits, and location.

**Key Features:**
- **AI-Driven Co-Founder Matching** – Uses data-based analysis to match founders based on skills, interests, and compatibility.
- **Data-Driven Insights & Recommendations** – Provides real-time recommendations and learns from user feedback.
- **Secure & Scalable Architecture** – Implements authentication, privacy controls, and scalable infrastructure.

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js, Tailwind CSS, Clerk (authentication), Framer Motion (animations)
- **Backend:** Django REST Framework, PostgreSQL, Redis (caching), JWT Authentication
- **Hosting:** Vercel (frontend), Railway (backend & database)

Originally developed as part of a **VinUniversity Elab client project**, the platform has been recognized for its innovation and has the potential to become a widely adopted startup networking tool.
